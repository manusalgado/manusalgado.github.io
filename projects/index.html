ORA

English practice copilot for interviews and meetings. The user speaks out loud; the app transcribes, analyzes clarity and structure, and suggests a stronger version of their answer in real time.

Stack & focus: React, TypeScript, voice APIs and language models for real-time analysis and suggestions. Auth and session state, responsive UI, and deployment on Vercel. Design centered on practice flows (interviews, planning, client calls) and actionable feedback.
